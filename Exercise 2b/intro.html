INTRODUCTION

Vision
To promote highly technical and Innovative Computer Professionals through excellence in teaching,
training and research.

Mission

To produce globally competent professionals, motivated to learn the emerging technologies and to
be innovative in solving real world problems.

To promote research activities among students and the members of faculty that could benefit
the society.
